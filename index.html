Frequently Asked Questions about Student Visa

A student visa is an official document that allows you to study in a foreign country legally. It is required by the government of your destination country to enroll in educational institutions.

We offer up-to-date visa information and application support for students applying to the UK, Canada, Australia, and Germany.

The application process varies by country, but generally involves submitting an acceptance letter from your chosen institution, proof of finances, and supporting documents. Our step-by-step guides walk you through each requirement.

Common documents include a valid passport, university acceptance letter, financial statements, proof of language proficiency, and recent photographs. Check our country-specific guides for detailed lists.

Processing times vary by country and time of year. It’s best to apply as early as possible—refer to our guides for estimated timelines for each destination.
